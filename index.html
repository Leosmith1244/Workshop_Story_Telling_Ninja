The Hollywood Hook

Objective: Teach how to craft a story opening that grabs attention instantly.

"Picture a dark theater, popcorn in hand, when a single, hypnotic image—a ticking clock—flashes. Your heart races. Why? Great hooks grab you in seconds."

Activity: Hook Blitz

Write and share a one-sentence story hook in Zoom chat.

Emotional Espionage

Objective: Show how to tap into primal emotions to hold attention.

"A 30-second ad about a lost puppy had millions in tears—its hypnotic pull came from fear and love." Emotional stories are remembered 65% more than facts.

Activity: Emotion Match

Match an emotion to a speech topic in breakout rooms.

The Plot Twist Payoff

Objective: Teach how to craft surprise endings that leave audiences obsessed.

"A speech ended, 'I quit my job.' Dull—until the speaker added, 'to climb Everest.' That hypnotic twist hooked us."

Activity: Twist Rewrite

Rewrite a boring speech ending with a twist in Zoom chat.

Improv Storytelling

Objective: Teach how improvisation strengthens storytelling.

"Two strangers weave a hypnotic story from random prompts—proof you can tell stories on the spot!"

Activity: Improv Relay

Build a group story in Zoom, passing it along.
